# Galilean moon imagery

The moon sprites are real spacecraft imagery served at 500 px for this project.

- `io.jpg`: NASA / JPL / University of Arizona, public domain, image PIA02308.
  Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Io_highest_resolution_true_color.jpg
- `europa.png`: NASA/JPL-Caltech/SwRI/MSSS/Kevin M. Gill, CC BY 2.0.
  Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Europa_-_Perijove_45_(cropped).png
- `ganymede.png`: NASA/JPL-Caltech/SwRI/MSSS/Kevin M. Gill, CC BY 2.0.
  Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Ganymede_-_Perijove_34_Composite.png
- `callisto.png`: Voyager imagery processed by zelario12, CC BY-SA 2.0.
  Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Callisto_VGR2_C2060635_OGB.png

No source image pixels were edited. Files are resized derivatives served by Wikimedia Commons.
